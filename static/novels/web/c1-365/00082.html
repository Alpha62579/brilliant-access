I finished my sewing and gained the singing skill and Velvet managed to get the sewing skill.  I gave Nia and Velvet the scarves I was working on, and their reactions made me feel a sense of pride.  Velvets an artistically embroidered whip-sword design, while Nias had fox and cat motifs.  By the end of the day, Nia was completely recovered so we were ready to go in the morning.
